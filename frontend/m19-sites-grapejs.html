FAQ

Questions, answered

Is AiMindShare really all-in-one?

Yes. CRM, sites, funnels, SEO, content, email, booking, payments, automation and AI agents all live in one platform, sharing one login, design system and data layer — so you can replace a whole stack of disconnected tools.

Can I use just one module?

Absolutely. Every module works standalone and opens as its own focused app with its own navigation — start with the AI Website Builder or CRM and add the rest whenever you're ready.

How do AI credits work?

One shared credit pool powers AI across every module — generating a site, writing a campaign or running an agent all draw from the same balance, so you never juggle separate AI subscriptions.

Can agencies manage multiple client workspaces?

Yes — the Agency plan includes unlimited multi-tenant workspaces, each fully isolated by row-level security, with brand kits, roles, permissions and audit logs per client.

Do I own my data?

Always. Your content and customer data are yours, isolated per workspace and exportable at any time.
