Questions

Answers before you ask

Do I need any coding experience?

None. Describe your business in plain language and AiMindShare drafts the whole site. Everything after is drag-and-drop — though a full code view is there if you want it.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes. On Growth and above, connect any custom domain with a CNAME and TXT record — DNS verification and SSL are handled automatically.

How is this different from a template builder?

Generation is driven by a structured Business Profile, so pages ship with correct SEO, schema and internal links — not just a pretty shell. You can also bulk-generate service and location pages that actually rank.

Is it built for agencies?

Absolutely. Manage many branded client sites from one place, with workspace template libraries, brand kits, roles and permissions, and per-site health scores and audit logs.

Do I own the sites I create?

Yes — your content and data are yours. Every workspace is isolated and secured, and you can export at any time.
